FAQ

Common Questions

Can I get a refund if I miss the event? +
No refunds are possible within 10 days of the event, as all arrangements (accommodation, travel, food) are booked in advance. If you notify us early (more than 10 days before), a full refund will be processed.
Will I become profitable after attending TFLCLUB? +
We don't claim to make any trader profitable. What we provide are the resources, environment, and opportunities that significantly increase your chances. The outcome depends on your consistency and application after the bootcamp.
Can I learn strategies within 5 days? +
Yes. You'll observe and learn multiple strategies from mentors whose style resonates with yours. The live trading sessions allow you to see strategies in action and ask questions in real-time.
Are there any discounts available? +
Yes! Join our trading tournaments — top 3 traders win scholarships: 1st place $500, 2nd place $300, 3rd place $150. Additionally, all other tournament participants receive an instant ₹5,000 discount on enrollment.
What happens after the season ends? +
You become a permanent part of the TFLCLUB ecosystem — priority access to future seasons, exclusive prop firm cashback (up to 100%), a dedicated traders resolution centre, and regular interaction through the community group.
Who is this NOT for? +
TFLCLUB is not for traders looking for a quick fix or guaranteed profits. It's for serious traders who are willing to put in the work, document their journey, and genuinely want to build discipline and a strong trading network.
